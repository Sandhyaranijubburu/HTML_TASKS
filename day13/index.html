OUR VALUES

Values
The principles of equality, ethics, fairness, social responsibility and respect are the values that we honor at Accenture. We put a great emphasis on people, and that is why we launched the Ability to Success program worldwide in 2019that helps our employees to grow professionally and develops new talents in the digital age. We support employees in starting up families and look for ways how to find work and family balance even after resumption of their work. We have a diverse work environment in which we actively support disabled workers, LGBT and others.

Benefits
At Accenture we provide many employee benefits. To name just a few, it is the fifth week of holidays, sick days, meal vouchers and employee discounts, but also many opportunities for further education, such as paid trainings or language courses. In addition, we offer a system of optional benefits for employees.
